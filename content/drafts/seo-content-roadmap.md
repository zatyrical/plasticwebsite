## Publishing principles

- Patient-facing, educational and factual.
- Avoid guarantees, superlatives, sensational claims, testimonials and before/after promises.
- Use Dr Sun’s objective credentials: Senior Consultant status, academic work, publications, dedicated lymphedema training, invited talks, reconstructive experience.
- Each procedure page should include: overview, who may be suitable, consultation assessment, treatment options, recovery, risks/limitations, FAQs and enquiry CTA.
- Articles should answer specific patient questions and link naturally to the relevant procedure page.
