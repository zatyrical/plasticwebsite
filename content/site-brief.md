# Dr Jeremy Sun personal website brief

## Positioning
Professional personal website for a Singapore plastic, reconstructive and aesthetic surgeon. Tone: premium, precise, academic, warm, compliance-safe.

## Compliance guardrails
- Avoid unverifiable superlatives such as “best”, “top”, “leading” unless tied to objective source.
- Avoid guaranteed outcomes or implied certainty.
- Before/after photos and patient testimonials require separate compliance review.
- Training photos should only be used if permission/consent is clear.
- Procedure pages should include suitability, alternatives, recovery, limitations and risks.

## Hero image
Use `/public/images/dr-jeremy-sun-hero.jpg`. The portrait has a dark background and emerald suit. Best treatment: right-side portrait, left-side copy, warm off-white background transitioning to deep black/emerald.
